Instinct Rehearsal Theory

  • According to this theory, dreams are part of humans' and animals' survival and development strategy.
  • By playing scary and possibly occurable instances, the mind "prepares" us to face the threats in real life.
  • In the stone age, these could include dreams about being attacked or chased by a mammoth and numerous more because in dreams, you imagination is the limit.
  • However, nowadays, humans have loads of newer problems that have evolved along with us. So even dreams like being rebuked, expecting the unexpected, and refusals are also to rehearse yourself for whatever could possibly happen. This usually includes dreams about "fight or flight" situations.
